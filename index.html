Discover Messina & Sicily

Explore the beautiful city of Messina and the stunning island of Sicily. From historic landmarks to Mediterranean beaches, discover what makes this region perfect for international students.

Messina

Messina is a beautiful port city located in northeastern Sicily. The city sits on the Strait of Messina, making it a natural gateway between Sicily and mainland Italy.

Key highlights:

  • Population: 230,000 people
  • Mediterranean climate year-round
  • Historic Messina Cathedral
  • Beautiful seaside location
  • Safe and student-friendly

Sicily

Sicily is the largest island in the Mediterranean Sea, known for its stunning landscapes, rich history, and incredible food. It's a dream destination for students seeking an authentic Italian experience.

What to explore:

  • Mount Etna - Active volcano
  • Beautiful beaches and clear waters
  • Ancient Greek ruins
  • Unique Sicilian cuisine
  • Charming historic towns

  • Friendly and welcoming locals

About VSAM

Vietnamese Students Association in Messina

VSAM - Vietnamese Students Association in Messina was established with the goal of bringing positive values to Vietnamese students living and studying in this beautiful city.

VSAM aims to build a united and thriving community through a series of activities that share Vietnamese-Italian culture, daily life, and the academic experiences of Vietnamese students in Messina.

We hope that VSAM's establishment will create a space that connects the Vietnamese community of students who have studied and are currently studying in Messina, as well as bring memorable experiences during their time living here.
